Things you can do

Include an event

We’d like to hear if you know of or are organizing an event you’d like to have us consider. Use our submission form..

Attend an event

Our calendar of Critical Conversations is growing by the week. You’re sure to find something of interest.

Write what you think

Want to be heard? Critical Conversations is open to publishing well-written, well-argued opinion pieces. We’ll consider audio or video submissions as well. Contact us.
